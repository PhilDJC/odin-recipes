So guys, after a long week working hard, eating right, and working on our craft

we need a little treat to get us into the next week. Here's my version of french toast

Ingredients:

  • 3x 4 inch thick slices of Brioche Bread
  • 4x Eggs
  • Peanut Butter
  • Whipped cream
Extras:
Tequila infused Maple Syrup

Recipe:

  1. Crack 4 eggs in a bowl and whisk
  2. Butter that Brioche
  3. Dunk that brioche in the egg mix, really SOAK it all up
  4. Fry that Brioche for 3 mins (or until golden)
  5. Flip that brioche over
  6. Fry for another 2 mins
  7. Take brioche out and put on plate to cool
  8. Spread on Peanut Butter and whipped cream and maple syrup
